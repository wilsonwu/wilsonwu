## Wilson Wu

Wilson Wu is a software engineering leader with nearly 20 years of experience in Cloud Native Enterprise Solutions and AI Business Applications, specializing in Containers, Microservices, DevOps, AI, and R&D team management.

He is a maintainer and member of top open source projects including Kubernetes, Istio, Contour, ArgoCD, and vLLM; a former Microsoft Evangelist and Philips Architect. Wilson is now focused on engineering management, leading AI/ML and LLM teams, specializing in AI Infra/AIOps, LLM Inference, and AI Gateway architectures.
